## Notes for implementation
- **Internal links**: once the blog module (see `BLOG_MODULE_PLAN.md`) is live, these should be real `<a href="/blog/[slug]">` links inserted into each post's body — not just a mental map. Build the full 25-post list first (even as drafts) so slugs exist before wiring cross-links, or link by title and backfill slugs once created.
- **External links**: always `rel="noopener"`, and prefer `.gov`, `.edu`, recognized health/tourism authorities, or Wikipedia over any commercial third party — keeps E-E-A-T (experience/expertise/authority/trust) signals clean and avoids inadvertently linking to a competitor.
- **GEO (local SEO)**: Pillar 3 is the primary local-search driver — make sure each of its posts naturally mentions "Rawai," "Nai Harn," and "Phuket" in the title, first paragraph, and image alt text, and consider adding LocalBusiness/Article structured data (the site already has a `jsonLdScript`/`spaSchema` helper in `lib/json-ld.js` to extend for blog posts).
- **Publishing order suggestion**: start with Pillar 5 (conversion posts) and Pillar 1 #1 (highest search volume), then fill in the rest — gets a working internal-link web sooner rather than publishing pillars in strict numerical order.
